n8n Workflow Automation

Project Summary

A regional manufacturer integrated Accounts Payable and receivable systems using n8n. Automated invoice routing and AR follow-ups reduced processing costs by 25% while keeping the call center updated in real time.

The Challenge

Manual coordination between AP and AR teams caused delays and inconsistent updates for the call center.

Our Solution

We built an n8n workflow that synchronized data across finance systems and triggered real-time notifications.

The Results

Processing costs dropped by 25% and call center agents received up-to-date account information.
