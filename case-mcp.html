MCP Governance

Project Summary

An insurance company adopted our Model Control Platform (MCP) to track AI performance and compliance. The platform provided dashboards for call center metrics and ensured auditability across AP and AR automation flows.

The Challenge

Management needed consistent governance of AI models used in financial workflows.

Our Solution

We deployed an MCP that logged model activity and visualized performance in real-time dashboards.

The Results

Compliance reporting became effortless and teams gained clear insight into model ROI.
