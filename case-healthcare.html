Healthcare Automation

Executive Summary

A regional healthcare network needed to streamline patient scheduling and insurance verification. By deploying an AI-driven workflow, ExpertTech eliminated manual phone follow-ups and reduced claim denials. Staff now have a holistic view of each patient’s journey, from appointment booking to payment processing.

The automation suite integrates seamlessly with existing electronic health record systems, ensuring compliance while dramatically improving throughput.

About the Client

The organization operates two hospitals and six outpatient clinics across three counties. Before partnering with ExpertTech, their administrative staff spent countless hours coordinating appointments, checking benefits, and chasing down paperwork from insurers.

The network wanted a modern solution that would minimize wait times and free clinicians to focus on care rather than data entry.

Challenge

Manual workflows led to double bookings, lengthy approval cycles, and frustrated patients. Insurance verification alone could take several days, delaying necessary treatments and creating revenue bottlenecks. Management sought a secure platform to consolidate these tasks and provide real-time updates to staff and patients alike.

Because of strict privacy regulations, any AI solution needed to handle protected health information carefully while integrating with legacy scheduling tools.

Technical Deep Dive

ExpertTech implemented a conversational agent integrated with the client’s EHR via API. Appointment requests are triaged automatically, and the system checks insurance eligibility through third-party services. OCR capabilities extract key data from uploaded documents, while a rules engine handles special cases like referrals.

Results & ROI

Automated reminders and streamlined verification reduced no-show rates by 20%. Claims are now submitted correctly on the first try, accelerating revenue by more than $1M in the first year. Patients appreciate faster response times, and staff morale has improved as tedious tasks disappear.

The network continues to expand the platform, adding chat-based follow-ups and medication refills.

Testimonial

“ExpertTech transformed our front-office operations. Patients get the answers they need right away, and our team spends more time delivering care,” said the Director of Operations.
