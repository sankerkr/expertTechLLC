Agentic Workflows

Project Summary

A telecom provider used agentic workflows to triage support tickets. Agents coordinate autonomously to classify issues, retrieve account data and escalate when needed, improving service levels and reducing call transfers.

The Challenge

High ticket volume made manual triage slow and error-prone.

Our Solution

We deployed a multi-agent workflow to classify requests, pull account information and escalate critical issues automatically.

The Results

Call transfers dropped sharply and service levels improved.
