A2A Collaboration

Executive Summary

ExpertTech deployed an Agent-to-Agent (A2A) collaboration framework for a telecom provider. Multiple AI agents now share context and solve routine issues autonomously, freeing human staff to handle complex requests. The solution processes over ten thousand interactions per week with a consistent 92% satisfaction rate.

The system seamlessly integrates with the existing CRM and scales effortlessly during peak call times.

About the Client

The client serves half a million subscribers across the Midwest. Rising call volume was straining their support team, yet hiring more agents wasn’t feasible. They sought an innovative approach to maintain service quality without dramatically expanding headcount.

ExpertTech worked closely with customer experience leaders to align automation goals with existing business processes.

Challenge

Support agents spent nearly 70% of their time on password resets, billing questions, and basic troubleshooting. This left little room for proactive outreach or retention efforts. Management needed a scalable way to deflect routine tickets while ensuring more complex issues were escalated quickly.

Any automation had to maintain conversation state across channels and integrate with internal account systems for accurate responses.

Technical Deep Dive

We orchestrated a network of specialized agents—billing, tech support, device activation—coordinated through a central knowledge store built on a vector database. An intent classifier routes each request to the appropriate agent, which then leverages retrieval-augmented generation to craft responses. A Redis-backed session service preserves context across chat and email channels.

Results & ROI

First-contact resolution improved by 35% within three months. Average response times dropped to under thirty seconds, saving an estimated $500k annually by reducing overtime costs and lowering agent attrition. Customer satisfaction scores jumped eight points.

The platform now serves as a foundation for additional automation projects across the organization.

Testimonial

“ExpertTech’s multi-agent system revolutionized how we handle support. Our team can finally focus on proactive outreach instead of constantly putting out fires,” said the Director of Customer Experience.
